⚔️ Learn everything about Jeddi, the legendary hero

In the vast universe of Legends of the World of Jeddi, one name echoes with strength and mystery: Jeddi. (grandfather) A tireless researcher and guardian of ancient tales, he travels through centuries in search of forgotten stories that have shaped our shared imagination.

Within the silent halls of the National Library of France, Jeddi dives into ancient manuscripts, deciphers words written in Old French, and brings back to life the legends passed down orally through generations.

His work is to rewrite these tales in the language of today, so everyone can understand them, feel them, and share them once more. But his journey doesn’t end there: Jeddi also translates these stories into English, Spanish, German and Italian, allowing legends from around the world to meet and converse.

He draws his sources from ancient books, online archives, and sites dedicated to the memory of peoples, patiently weaving a bridge between past and present. A long, passionate, and respectful labor so that the voices of yesterday continue to inspire those of tomorrow.
